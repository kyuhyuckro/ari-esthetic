About Ari Kang

Ari Kang

Licensed Esthetician · Founder, ARI Esthetic

Ari Kang is a licensed esthetician with over five years of experience, trained in Seoul and Busan, South Korea. Her practice is grounded in the Korean approach to skincare, one that treats skin not as a surface to be corrected, but as a reflection of overall wellness that deserves consistent, expert attention.

With a Bachelor's degree in Business from Korea, studies abroad in China, and a Master's degree in Business from Missouri, her global background shapes the way ARI Esthetic is built and run. That same sense of care and intentionality carries into every treatment she provides.

At ARI Esthetic, every treatment begins with a thorough skin assessment. She believes that real results come from truly understanding each client's skin history and then bringing together the right technique, knowledge, and care to make it happen.

Specialty

Problematic Skin

From chronic acne to reactive and sensitive skin, Ari works to understand the root cause and treat it with a personalized, results-driven approach.

Specialty

Anti-Aging Care

Through skin barrier strengthening and targeted treatments, Ari works to restore firmness, improve texture, and bring back a natural, youthful radiance.

Signature

Facial Contouring

Rooted in Korean esthetic tradition, her contouring work sculpts and lifts the face naturally through skilled manual technique, without needles or fillers.
